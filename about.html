Who We Are

A Name Built on Trust in Real Estate

Treasure Realties was born from a simple yet powerful idea — that buying or renting a property should be a joyful experience, not a stressful one. Founded along the Yamuna Expressway corridor, we set out to bridge the gap between ambitious homebuyers and the complex world of real estate.

We are a boutique real estate consultancy offering personalised, hands-on service. Unlike large agencies where you are just a ticket number, at Treasure Realties you work directly with experienced advisors who understand your needs and fight for your best interests.

From our first consultation to your final property handover, we remain by your side — navigating legalities, negotiating prices, and ensuring you never feel alone in the process.

Meet Our Founder

Bhagat Singh

Founder & Principal Real Estate Advisor

A Visionary Leader

Turning Real Estate Dreams Into Reality

Bhagat Singh is the founder and driving force behind Treasure Realties. With a deep passion for real estate and an unwavering commitment to his clients, he has personally overseen every deal the firm has closed — ensuring the highest standards of transparency and service.

His journey began with a profound understanding of the untapped potential along the Yamuna Expressway corridor. He saw what others missed: an emerging world-class township belt backed by infrastructure investments that would define NCR's growth for decades.

Today, Bhagat Singh leverages that foresight and market acumen to help clients — from first-time homebuyers to seasoned investors — make informed, profitable decisions. His philosophy is simple: a client's gain is his greatest reward.

When not closing deals, Bhagat Singh stays ahead of market trends, attending industry conclaves, studying policy changes, and building relationships with top developers to offer his clients an exclusive edge.
