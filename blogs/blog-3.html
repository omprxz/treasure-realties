Price vs Value: Why Appraisal Thinking Helps Buyers

In many transactions, the asking price and market value are not identical. Appraisal frameworks worldwide use comparables, property condition, and utility to estimate value more objectively.

Why mismatch happens

  • Buyer urgency or seller pressure affects negotiation.
  • Incomplete market knowledge leads to overpaying.
  • Premium features are priced emotionally, not realistically.

How to apply this in practice

  • Compare at least 3 to 5 similar recent transactions.
  • Adjust for floor, facing, age, and amenities.
  • Evaluate neighborhood demand depth for 3 to 5 years.
  • Check if rental yield justifies the ticket size.

Practical takeaway: Great deals come from value clarity, not only discount percentages.
