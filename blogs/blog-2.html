First-Time Buyer Checklist for Safer Property Decisions

First-home decisions can feel overwhelming. Use this 10-point checklist before paying token money:

  1. Define budget using comfortable EMI range, not maximum eligibility.
  2. Shortlist micro-locations based on travel time, not just distance.
  3. Check builder reputation and completion history.
  4. Verify project approvals and sanctioned plan documents.
  5. Review title chain and ownership history.
  6. Ask for encumbrance status and pending dues clarity.
  7. Check possession timeline with penalty clauses.
  8. Compare maintenance model and hidden charges.
  9. Evaluate resale and rental demand in that pocket.
  10. Get all payment terms in writing before transfer.

Practical takeaway: A disciplined checklist reduces legal, financial, and emotional mistakes.
