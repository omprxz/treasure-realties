Service 01

Property Buying

Whether you're purchasing your first home, upgrading to a larger space, or making a strategic investment, Treasure Realties ensures the process is transparent, stress-free, and profitable. We specialise in properties across the Yamuna Expressway corridor — from affordable apartments to premium luxury villas and investment plots.

Our team handles everything: curating verified listings based on your budget and preferences, arranging physical site visits, negotiating the best price, and guiding you through the entire documentation and registration process.

Verified Listings — Every property is RERA-checked and legally cleared before we present it to you
Legal Assistance — Full support with documentation, title verification, stamp duty, and registration
Guided Site Visits — We personally accompany you to every site and offer an honest, unbiased assessment
Home Loan Facilitation — Partnership with leading banks for pre-approved loans at competitive rates
Price Negotiation — Our market expertise ensures you always get the best possible deal

Rental Services

Finding the right rental — or the right tenant — shouldn't be a gamble. Treasure Realties offers comprehensive rental management services for both landlords and tenants, ensuring a smooth, transparent, and mutually beneficial experience.

For landlords, we handle tenant sourcing, background verification, rental agreement drafting, and ongoing tenant management. For tenants, we offer a curated selection of residential and commercial rentals with flexible terms and zero brokerage complications.

Residential Rentals — Flats, apartments, and independent floors for families and working professionals
Commercial Rentals — Office spaces, retail shops, and commercial units across the Yamuna Expressway zone
Flexible Terms — Short-term and long-term rental agreements customised to your requirements
Tenant Verification — Thorough background and reference checks to protect landlord interests
