About ACE Divino

ACE Divino is a premium residential project by ACE Group located in Sector 1, Greater Noida West (Noida Extension). The project spans over 12 acres and offers meticulously designed 2 and 3 BHK apartments with modern aesthetics, functional layouts, and an extensive array of lifestyle amenities.

The development features 9 high-rise towers with a total of 2,400 units across G+24 floors. Each apartment is designed with optimal space utilization, cross-ventilation, and abundant natural light. The project is RERA registered under UPRERAPRJ7233 and has seen consistent demand from both end-users and investors.

ACE Divino is strategically positioned near the FNG (Faridabad–Noida–Ghaziabad) Expressway, providing excellent connectivity to Noida, Greater Noida, and Delhi. It is 20 minutes from the proposed Noida International Airport at Jewar, making it an ideal long-term investment.
