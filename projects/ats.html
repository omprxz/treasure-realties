About ATS Allure

ATS Allure is a premium residential project by ATS Infrastructure Ltd., strategically located in Sector 22D along the Yamuna Expressway, Greater Noida. The project is spread across approximately 8.48 acres and comprises 1,146 thoughtfully designed apartments distributed across 8 towers, each rising to 23 floors — offering residents sweeping views and excellent privacy.

Positioned perfectly along the Yamuna Expressway corridor, ATS Allure is positioned just minutes from the upcoming Noida International Airport (Jewar), the Buddh International Formula 1 Circuit, and Pari Chowk — making it an outstanding choice for both end-users and investors seeking long-term capital appreciation.

The project is now ready to move, making it a safe, legally sound purchase with immediate rental income potential. Its proximity to educational institutions like Gautam Buddha University, along with world-class in-house amenities, makes it ideal for families and working professionals alike.
