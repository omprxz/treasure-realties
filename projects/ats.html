About ATS Knightsbridge

ATS Knightsbridge is widely regarded as one of India's most exclusive and prestigious residential developments. Located in Sector 124, Noida, along the Noida–Greater Noida Expressway, this architectural marvel by ATS Group offers super-luxury 4 and 5 BHK residences that redefine the standard of upscale living in Delhi NCR.

The project is spread across 12.5 acres and features just 150 exclusive residences across three high-rise towers, ensuring privacy, exclusivity, and an elite community. Each apartment spans between 5,450 and 6,500 sq.ft., offering generous room sizes, private terraces, home theatres, study rooms, and staff quarters as standard features.

ATS Knightsbridge is fitted with internationally sourced branded fixtures — Kohler/Grohe bathrooms, Gaggenau kitchen appliances, Duravit sanitary ware, and Legrand switches throughout. The clubhouse spans 30,000 sq.ft. with a business centre, spa, salon, fine dining restaurant, and concierge services, providing residents with a 5-star hotel-like living experience 365 days a year.
