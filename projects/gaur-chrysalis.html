About Gaur Chrysalis

Gaur Chrysalis is a signature luxury residential development by the Gaurs Group in Sector 22D, Yamuna Expressway, Greater Noida — located adjacent to the established Gaur Yamuna City township. Spread across approximately 12 acres, the project features G+34 high-rise towers, developed in phases, with Phase 1 comprising 7 towers of premium 3 and 4 BHK residences.

The project was conceived with a distinctive "butterfly chrysalis" design philosophy by the renowned architect Hafeez Contractor — delivering an architectural identity that is bold, contemporary, and visually striking. The glass-facade clubhouse spanning 75,000 sq.ft. is one of the largest and most well-equipped residential clubhouses in the Yamuna Expressway corridor.

Gaur Chrysalis offers residents a premium lifestyle powered by Olympic pools, state-of-the-art fitness zones, yoga studios, sports arenas, landscaped greens, and integrated smart specifications including modular kitchens, Italian-tiled surfaces, and air conditioning in select units — delivering a truly elevated residential experience.
