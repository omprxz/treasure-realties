Project Overview

About Nimbus The Palm Village

Nimbus The Palm Village is a distinctive low-rise residential project by Nimbus Group, located at GH-03, Sector 22A along the Yamuna Expressway, Greater Noida. Unlike conventional high-rise towers, this project embraces a unique low-density, low-rise (G+4 floors) design philosophy — offering residents a quieter, more private, and community-oriented lifestyle surrounded by ample green spaces.

The project is RERA registered under UPRERAPRJ558356/04/2024 (Phase 1) with planned possession by March 2028. Spread across approximately 11–12 acres, Nimbus The Palm Village offers 1 BHK studio-style apartments (525–535 sq.ft.) and spacious 3 BHK low-rise homes (2,100–2,200 sq.ft.) — with all units designed to be fully Vastu-compliant.

The project is strategically positioned near the upcoming Noida International Airport (Jewar Airport), the iconic Buddh International Circuit, and leading educational institutions including Gautam Buddha University — making it an excellent choice for investors and families seeking a serene yet well-connected home along the Yamuna Expressway.
