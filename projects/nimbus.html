About Nimbus The Golden Palm

Nimbus The Golden Palm is a premium residential project by Nimbus Group situated in Sector 168, Noida, directly on the Noida–Greater Noida Expressway. The project offers elegantly designed 2 and 3 BHK apartments across 9 towers of G+25 floors, spread over 10 acres with a total of 1,840 units.

The project's name is inspired by its signature feature — palm-lined internal roads, tropical landscaping, and a resort-style central clubhouse that recreates the experience of a 5-star resort for everyday living. Over 60% of the project area is dedicated to green spaces, water features, and open recreational zones.

Nimbus The Golden Palm is RERA-registered and offers excellent connectivity — it is 10 minutes from the Sector 137 Metro Station (Aqua Line), 15 minutes from Sector 18 Noida, and approximately 25 minutes from the upcoming Noida International Airport at Jewar. The combination of location, green living, and resort-style amenities makes it one of Sector 168's most sought-after addresses.
