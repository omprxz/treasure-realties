About Godrej Tropical Isle

Godrej Tropical Isle redefines luxury living in the heart of Noida's most coveted zone — Sector 146, abutting the Noida–Greater Noida Expressway. Developed by Godrej Properties, one of India's most trusted real estate brands, this project offers ultra-premium 2, 3, and 4 BHK sky residences spread across 6.46 acres with a total of 630 apartments.

The project is inspired by the concept of a tropical paradise — with over 70% open space, dense green corridors, a water-body central zone, and over 30 curated amenities spread across multiple levels. Notably, it features dedicated sky-level amenities at every 10th floor, offering residents private pool decks, sky lounges, and BBQ zones with panoramic views of the Noida skyline.

Positioned as an IGBC Green-certified project, Godrej Tropical Isle incorporates sustainable design features including rainwater harvesting, energy-efficient lighting, double-glazed windows, and solar panels — ensuring both environmental responsibility and long-term cost savings for residents.
