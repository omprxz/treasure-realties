About Supertech Eco Village

Supertech Eco Village is an eco-conscious residential township developed by Supertech Ltd. in Sector 1, Greater Noida West — one of NCR's fastest-growing residential corridors. The project is designed around a sustainability ethos, incorporating solar energy provisions, rainwater harvesting, organic waste management, and extensive green cover across its 23-acre campus.

The project offers 1, 2, and 3 BHK apartments across 14 towers of G+20 floors, with total 4,200 units across multiple phases. The eco-village concept creates a green breathing environment — with 70% of the total area dedicated to open spaces, tree cover, parks, and water features. Eco Village has a 28,000 sq.ft. clubhouse, 2 pools, and 40+ amenities including a dedicated organic garden for residents.

RERA-registered and with select phases Ready to Move, Supertech Eco Village offers a unique combination of sustainable living, affordable pricing, and strong connectivity — located on the FNG Expressway and just 20 km from Jewar International Airport.
