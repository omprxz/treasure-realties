About Eldeco Acclaim

Eldeco Acclaim is a prestigious residential township developed by the Eldeco Group, one of North India's most reputed real estate developers. Located in Sector 119, Noida, the project extends over 14.5 acres and offers meticulously crafted 2 and 3 BHK apartments across 10 towers of G+22 floors.

The project was designed by a renowned architectural firm and offers a distinctive modern aesthetic. Eldeco Acclaim features a 3-acre central green park, a 30,000 sq.ft. air-conditioned clubhouse, 2 full-size swimming pools, and over 50 lifestyle amenities. The project is RERA approved and fully delivered — with active resale and rental opportunities available.
