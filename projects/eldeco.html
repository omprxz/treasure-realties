About Eldeco Yamuna Expressway Sector 22D

Eldeco Yamuna Expressway Sector 22D is a premium residential development by the Eldeco Group in Sector 22D, Yamuna Expressway, Greater Noida. Registered under RERA (UPRERAPRJ752382/09/2025), this project offers thoughtfully designed 3 BHK apartments and duplex penthouses, catering to families and investors seeking quality and value in the high-growth Yamuna Expressway corridor.

The project features 3 BHK unit sizes ranging from 1,550 to 1,850 sq.ft. and duplex penthouses from 2,500 to 2,800 sq.ft., priced at a base rate of approximately ₹9,000–₹10,000 per sq.ft. Total prices range from ₹1.4 Cr to ₹3.3 Cr+, offering excellent value in one of NCR's most rapidly developing real estate corridors.

Eldeco Group has a strong legacy of delivering premium residential projects across Delhi NCR for over three decades — synonymous with quality construction, timely delivery, and exceptional after-sales service. This new development in Sector 22D continues this tradition with its premium design, modern amenities, and strategic location near the upcoming Jewar International Airport.
