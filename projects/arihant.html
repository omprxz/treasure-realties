Project Overview

About Arihant Seasons

Arihant Seasons is a landmark luxury residential project by Arihant Buildcon, located in Sector 22D along the Yamuna Expressway, Greater Noida. The project offers premium 3, 4, and 5 BHK high-rise apartments, setting a new standard for upscale living in this rapidly developing corridor. It is fully RERA registered under UPRERAPRJ974543/02/2026.

With planned possession in January 2031, Arihant Seasons is positioned as an early-entry investment opportunity in one of NCR's most sought-after emerging residential zones. Its pricing of approximately ₹11,330 per sq.ft. reflects the premium quality of construction and specifications offered.

The project benefits from excellent location advantages: proximity to the upcoming Noida International Airport (Jewar), the Buddh International Circuit, major universities, and the arterial Yamuna Expressway providing direct connectivity to Noida, Delhi, and Agra. A modern clubhouse, landscaped green areas, and smart security systems complete the Arihant Seasons experience.
