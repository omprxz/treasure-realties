About Arihant Arden

Arihant Arden is a well-planned residential project by Arihant Buildcon in Sector 1, Greater Noida West. It offers smartly designed 1, 2, and 3 BHK apartments, catering to first-time home buyers and young families seeking a balance of affordability and quality across 8 acres with 5 high-rise towers of G+22 floors.

The project is RERA-registered under UPRERAPRJ6812 and is Ready to Move for select phases. Arihant Arden boasts a 20,000 sq.ft. clubhouse, landscaped gardens, jogging track, gymnasium, and a dedicated children's play area. The township has excellent connectivity via the Noida–Greater Noida Expressway and the FNG Expressway, placing it within 20 minutes of major employment hubs.

With 1,600 families already residing in completed phases, Arihant Arden is an established, vibrant community with functional infrastructure and active society management — making it extremely popular for both end-users and rental investors.
