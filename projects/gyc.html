About Gaur Yamuna City

Gaur Yamuna City is one of the most ambitious integrated township projects along the Yamuna Expressway, developed by the renowned Gaur Group. Spread across approximately 250 acres, this township is designed to offer a complete lifestyle ecosystem encompassing premium residential apartments, high-street retail, commercial offices, schools, hospitals, and recreational zones.

Strategically located in Sector 19 along the Yamuna Expressway, it enjoys superb connectivity to Noida, Greater Noida, Agra, and the upcoming Noida International Airport (Jewar) — positioned just 10 km from the project site. The township has been developed in multiple phases with RERA-registered towers ensuring full legal compliance and buyer protection.

The project features landscaped gardens spread over 60% of the total area, a world-class clubhouse of 45,000 sq. ft., over 50 lifestyle amenities, dedicated sports zones, senior citizen areas, kids' play zones, and 24/7 security with CCTV surveillance. GYC is a self-sufficient city within a city, designed for modern urban families looking for quality, convenience, and value appreciation.
