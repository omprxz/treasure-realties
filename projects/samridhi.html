About Samridhi Grand Avenue

Samridhi Grand Avenue is a prominent residential township by Samridhi Group in Sector 1, Noida Extension (Greater Noida West). The project offers generously sized 2 and 3 BHK apartments across 8 towers spread over 12 acres, providing home buyers with spacious living in a clean, green environment.

Samridhi Grand Avenue is notable for its large floor plates — 2 BHK starts at 1,050 sq.ft. — considerably larger than most competing projects in the area. The project is RERA-registered and has active ready-to-move units available. It features a grand 25,000 sq.ft. clubhouse, 3 swimming pools, and over 35 amenities for residents.
