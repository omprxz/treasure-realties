About Accord The Icon

Accord The Icon is a distinctive mixed-use development by Accord Group in Sector Chi 5, Greater Noida — one of the most rapidly developing commercial corridors in the NCR. The project comprises premium 2 and 3 BHK residential towers alongside a dedicated commercial block featuring Grade A office spaces, retail high-street, and food court — creating a true live-work-play environment.

The residential segment spans 9 acres with 6 towers of G+19 floors, offering 1,080 units. All apartments feature smart layouts with cross-ventilation, premium finishes, and access to a 22,000 sq.ft. clubhouse with 35+ amenities. The commercial segment adds 200,000 sq.ft. of Grade A leasable area across 2 office towers with flexible floor plates catering to IT, BFSI, and co-working operators.

Located adjacent to the Greater Noida Expressway, Accord The Icon provides exceptional visibility, accessibility, and footfall — making it one of the few projects in Greater Noida offering both residential and commercial investment opportunities within a single gated campus.
