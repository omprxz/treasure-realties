About Purvanchal Royal City

Purvanchal Royal City is one of the largest integrated townships in Greater Noida West by Purvanchal Group — a 30-year old developer with 40,000+ delivered units. Spanning 185 acres, it includes residential towers, a shopping centre, dedicated school, multi-speciality clinic, and a large sports complex.

The project offers 2, 3, and 4 BHK apartments across multiple phases with RERA registration. Select Ready to Move phases have OC received with active RWA. Excellent location near FNG Expressway and Bisrakh Road, approximately 22 km from the upcoming Jewar International Airport.
