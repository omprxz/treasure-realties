Where We Operate

Areas We Serve

Our primary focus is the Yamuna Expressway corridor — one of India's most rapidly developing real estate zones, stretching from Greater Noida to Agra.

Noida International Airport

The upcoming Noida International Airport (Jewar Airport) is set to be one of Asia's largest airports, dramatically boosting property values and connectivity across the Yamuna Expressway zone.

Infrastructure Growth

Film City, DMIC industrial nodes, and world-class infrastructure projects are transforming this corridor into a premier urban hub with unprecedented investment potential.

Exceptional Connectivity

6-lane expressway, Metro extension, and proximity to Delhi NCR make daily commuting seamless — ideal for both end-users and investors.

Investment Potential

Property values along the Yamuna Expressway have shown consistent appreciation, making it one of NCR's best bets for long-term returns.
